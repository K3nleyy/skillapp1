Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How does the skill exchange work?

Users can offer a skill and, in return, learn another skill—no money involved, just knowledge!

2. How do I sign up?

Click the Sign Up button on the homepage and follow the prompts.

3. How do I offer or request a skill?

Go to the Dashboard, select Offer a Skill or Learn a Skill, and provide the necessary details.
